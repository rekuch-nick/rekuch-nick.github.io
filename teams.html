Once you unlock Team Registration Boards, you'll encounter one in most rooms. You can use a team's board to sign up for that team.

Each team will grant and deduct favor points for different actions, and you can spend favor points at that team's board to use that team's special powers.

Once you're a member of a particular team, there's a 50% chance each team board you encounter will belong to your team, and a 50% chance it'll belong to one of the other 3 teams.

You can change teams any time you find a different team's board, but you'll lose half of your favor points each time you do.

The PackRats are fans who prize exploration and speed above everything else. While on this team you'll gain favor for finding secret traps, doors, or chests; and each time you level up. You'll lose favor anytime you spend long enough in a single room that the troll shows up.
-50 Favor Gain a Potion.
-30 Favor Knock down all the walls in the room.
-90 Favor Turn up to 10 monsters into coins.
-50 Favor Learn about some of the secret rooms.
The WarPigs are fans of battle. While on this team you'll gain favor every time you take damage from a monster's attack. You'll lose favor each time you drink a potion or pick up a book.
-20 Favor Slow each monster you can see, so that you can strike first.
-20 Favor Cure Poison.
-80 Favor Improve the bonus on your weapon, but not to a higher score than +10.
-50 Favor Learn about some of the secret rooms.
The BookWorms are fans of magic spells. While on this team you'll gain favor each time you cast any spell. You'll lose favor each time you kill a monster that looks like a wizard.
-25 Favor Gain 20 MP.
-100 Favor Call a book to the current room. This ability will fail if there's already a book in the room.
-25 Favor Heal all of your HP.
-50 Favor Learn about the books.
The FreeBirds are fans that want to see rules broken and limits ignored. While on this team you'll gain favor each time you teleport or destroy a wall tile. You'll lose favor each time you get de-buffed with Poison, Slippery, Pertified, or Thorns.
-40 Favor Open any locked doors, chests, or stairways in the current room.
-100 Favor Summon up to 4 treasure chests.
-10 Favor Call the Troll.
-50 Favor Learn about the uses of the Power Orbs.
