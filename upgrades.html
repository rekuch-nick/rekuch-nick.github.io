After each run you'll count your winnings and possibly receive new UPGRADE POINTS. Spend these on various special powers each run to improve your chances. You can reallocate these every run, so don't worry about wasting them. Try all kinds of combinations!

Since all the upgrades don't appear until you've played for a while, you might want to be surprised by them. If you're sure you are ready to see the descriptions you can click on any of the upgrades below:

Hardy Gain +4 HP per level.
Advanced Reading Find 50% more books.
(Normally you'd find about 1 book every 4 rooms.)
Adventuring Kit Start the run with 2 keys, four bombs, and a potion.
Fast Gain +1 Speed.
Osmosis Gain +2 MP per level.
Eagle Eye Gain +2 Perception per level.
Sword Master Gain +2 Atk.
Famous You'll find a Team Registration Board on each floor.
(The Team Registration Boards let you sign up for different teams that grant special powers in exchange for performing actions that team likes.)
Swimming Entering water will no longer cause a new monster to spawn.
Boss Hunter You can encounter Guardian Monsters, but will encounter slightly more random hazards.
(Beating Guardian Monsters grants Power Orbs and special items.)
Storm Caller Begin the run already knowing the Bolt spell (Book 1) and with 4 bonus MP.
(The 4 bonus MP is 4 fewer MP Books you'll encounter- this perk means you'll need to find 5 less books to know every spell.)
Key Keeper Opening a door will not consume a key. You still need to have one.
(Opening a treasure chest will still consume a key.)
Coal Walker Take half damage from fire, and one third damage from lava.
Caster's Kit Start with a +2 Cape of Power that grants bonus MP, and a Staff of the Magi, that lets you cast all 6 of the basic spells.
(If you replace the staff you'll lose any spells you haven't read the book for.)
Collector Each time you equip a new piece of gear, you'll permanently gain +3 HP. You're also 20% more likely to find power bombs.
Quick Draw Each time you enter a new room you will gain first-strike and power attack buffs as if you had cast Blur 2 and Might 2. If your first attack in a room defeats a monster, it will drop a coin.
Dare Devil If you manage to find and beat a Guardian Monster while still level 1 you'll instantly gain 8 levels, 500 gold, and your team favor will be set to 100.
(This perk is good for rushing though the early parts of the game after you already have a lot of upgrades.)
Bushwacker Walking through tall grass will no longer stir up bugs.
King's Legacy Begin the run with +4 Midas Fist, an item that makes monster drop coins when you beat them.
(This perk lets you play more like a typical RPG where you fight monsters to level up.)
MP Pincher All of your spells cost 2 less MP.
Treasure Hunter One more treasure will be placed on each floor.
Beastly Gain +20 HP.
(While this eventually gives less total HP than Hardy, if gives them all right away. Of course, you could always use both for maximum HP.)
Orb Drinker Level up each time you collect a Power Orb.
(While the first 10 or so levels can come fairly quickly, you quickly need more and more XP to level up. The highest levels around 20 can (practically) only be reached with this perk.)
Stealthy Gain +1 Stealth. (Stealth is the number of steps you can take before a new monster appears.)
Eldritch T.V. Star Gain Regen stacks every time you enter a new room equal to that room's number.
Every 15th floor will have a valuable Gem worth 25 + (room number * 1.2) gold.
Each gem you pick up gives you +12 HP, 6 MP, 2 Atk, and 2 Perception.
Orbless Begin the run with zero Orbs, no matter how many you've already found.
Beating the game with this perk is the ultimate challenge!
(It is also the best way to make use of the Orb Drinker Perk and Ghost Armor.)
Collector Specter When you equip a piece of gear in each slot a dangerous ghost will appear to block your progress.
If you beat the ghost it will drop a special armor than improves in power each time you pick up an orb.
